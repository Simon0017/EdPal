What EdPal Does

Built for learners, not spreadsheets.

EdPal combines structured assessments with intelligent career matching so that the insights stay with the learner.

Adaptive Questionnaires

Create multi-step assessments with MCQ, ranking, numeric, and open-text question types. Questions adapt to time limits and scoring rules you define.

Timed & Tracked

Learners can start, pause, and resume attempts. Every session is timestamped and scored automatically — no manual marking required.

Instant Analytics

Scores, percentiles, pass/fail outcomes and subscore breakdowns are calculated the moment a learner submits. Results can be emailed automatically.

Career Matching

Tag-based career recommendations surface the most relevant paths based on a learner's cumulative performance across subjects and interests.

Staff & Learner Portals

Separate dashboards for administrators who create and monitor assessments and for learners who track their own progress and results.

Version Control

Every questionnaire edit creates a new version automatically — old attempts remain linked to the original version so results stay accurate over time.
